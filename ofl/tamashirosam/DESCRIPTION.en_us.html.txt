Tamashiro is a sans-serif typeface with rounded corners and a weight between regular and bold.  
It was created to be used in books authored by H. M. Tamashiro, following recommendations from the Brazilian Ministry of Education (MEC) for inclusive publishing.  
Designed to support smooth reading flow and reduce visual fatigue.
